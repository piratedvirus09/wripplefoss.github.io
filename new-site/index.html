What is open-source?

Open source refers to code that is available for free, allowing modification and redistribution by anyone for reusability and improvement. Popular examples include VLC, GIMP and LibreOffice.

Why WrippleFOSS

WrippleFOSS helps students to bring their ideas to life by guiding them in making amazing open source, team-based projects. We provide mentor support to the interested students, who guide the students through the hurdles in development.
